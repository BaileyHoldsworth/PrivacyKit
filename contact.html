Report a Bug or Issue

Found a problem with one of our tools? We take bugs seriously and want to fix them quickly. When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Which tool you were using (e.g., Password Generator, QR Code Generator)
  • What you were trying to do when the issue occurred
  • What went wrong or what behavior you experienced
  • Your browser name and version (e.g., Chrome 120, Firefox 121)
  • Your device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

Send bug reports to privacykitwebsite@gmail.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line.
